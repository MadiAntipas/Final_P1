Learn more about the Parking systems work in Wellington.

Parking in Wellington city has changed in the last year as the Wellington City Council decided to make Wellingtonians pay for parking on the weekends, like one would during the week.

There are multiple ways to pay for parking in and around Wellington City, Parking Meters, Pay by Space and PayMyPark are some of the few ways in which people can pay for their parking.

Like in all of the photos shown parking meters can be found all around the Wellington city area.
